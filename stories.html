When oomf and I witnessed a REAL gangstalk! (;¬∞ „É≠¬∞)

This all happened a few weeks back when I met up with my best friend in town. We agreed to meet up and go for a nice walk along the sea, then go back in to town and visit a pub.
The walk was really nice! I bought some cigarettes before we left and then we started on our way. The weather was lovely, super hot, super sunny. The tide was in too so we got to see a very pretty landscape!
Halfway through the walk we stopped off at an inn for some lunch. My friend had mac and cheese (his usual) with cajun fries, and I got cajun fries with onion rings. We both got a pint of pepsi and enjoyed the view.
We walked a little farther, sharing stories and our favourite songs along the way. Oomf played some cosmic metal and some obscure ambient black metal, and I showed him some funeral doom and some prog rock.
We made our way back in to town, exhausted and panting for a drink. We finally made it to a pub and we both got a couple drinks each.. Ive never been so grateful for water! (Ôø£ÔπÉÔø£) It wasn't long until the work day was finished, and since it was sunny the pub started to pile up. Luckily we were sat outside and a bit far out from the crowd.
This didn't stop the usual suspects from coming in. We didn't have a choice in whether or not we listened in to their conversation they were that loud. Spouting the usual normie things, we ignored most of it.
It wasn't until one of the men started to talk about work, he was saying how "work is evil" and "we shouldn't work at all", which was pretty based. My friend and I looked at each other and laughed at this, thankfully they didn't notice.
Things went quiet for some time, then a middle aged man walked in to the pub, and sat right in the doorway, staring at the usual suspects. Immediately, the man talking about work stood up and started yelling about how that man was a fed and how "he follows me in to every pub I go to".
They left then and there and we did too laughing as we went, remarking that we both witnessed a "real gangstalk". We then made our way to the shops before relaxing outside the train station.
